Benchmark Scope

Multimodal Temporal Reasoning With Visual Evidence

MMTR collects tasks where temporal signals must be interpreted together with visual evidence: static images, radiology images, financial charts, geospatial maps, egocentric videos, robot videos, driving videos, and industrial multi-camera recordings.

4video-grounded domains

Static Visual Evidence

Image and Chart Grounded Tasks

Each example pairs visual context with an aligned time series and a concrete target output.

PixelRec

Cover-aware interaction forecasting

Given an item cover, title metadata, and daily interaction counts, forecast the next six daily values.

Sample
pixelrec_test200_6_0c20bebda4
Context
Sports Mix item: "Best of GoPro POV 2021" style mountain biking content.
Target
Predict 2022-03-10 to 2022-03-15 interaction counts.
Reference
3, 5, 18, 5, 1, 1

MIMIC

Clinical outcome classification

Use chest X-ray images, a radiology report, and vital signs from a lookback window to answer a discharge question.

Sample
mimic_disch_test_2_f93aee762d
Question
Will the patient be discharged within the next 48 hours after the anchor time?
Options
A. Yes. B. No.
Reference
A

S&P 500

Financial forecasting with chart evidence

Forecast future prices from a candlestick image, recent news summaries, and historical close values.

Sample
sp500_test200_1_acfbf19108
Asset
AA, 2024 H1 historical chart.
Target
Forecast 12 trading days from 2024-07-01 to 2024-07-17.
Reference
Starts at 39.35 and ends at 36.48.

Terra

Geospatial multivariate forecasting

Forecast climate variables for a 1 degree by 1 degree grid cell using static geophysical layers and 100 days of multivariate history.

Sample
terra_test200_1_1ccbc81d48
Grid
10N_166E, Pacific Ocean cell.
Variables
lwd, precipitation, pressure, relative humidity, specific humidity, temperature, wind.
Target
Forecast 20 daily multivariate rows from 2023-12-12 to 2023-12-31.

Video and Sensor Grounded Tasks

AgiBot

Robot anomaly-cause classification

Determine the anomaly category from a robot shelf-manipulation video, step sequence, and arm-position sensor traces.

Instruction
Place pink cans and orange sausage sets from the shelf into the shopping cart.
Options
A paused. B meaningless operation. C fall. D other.
Reference
D
